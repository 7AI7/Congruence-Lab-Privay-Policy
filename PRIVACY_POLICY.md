## 4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

To operate the JITO Youth Event Bot, we use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **WhatsApp Business API (Meta)** | Message delivery and end-to-end encryption | Phone numbers, message content | [WhatsApp Privacy Policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy) |

- **OpenAI Processing:** We send ONLY message text to OpenAI for AI processing. We do NOT send your name, phone number, email, hotel details, or any personally identifying information to OpenAI.
- **Self-Hosted Infrastructure:** n8n and PostgreSQL run on our controlled servers. No external companies have access to your data through these systems.
- **No Marketing Partners:** We do not share your data with advertising networks, marketing platforms, or data brokers.

## 7. HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION SAFE?

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

### Technical Safeguards

- **End-to-End Encryption:** All WhatsApp messages are encrypted in transit (provided by WhatsApp)
- **Database Encryption:** PostgreSQL database uses encryption at rest
- **Access Controls:** Only authorized Congruence Labs technical team can access data
- **Secure Hosting:** Infrastructure hosted on secure servers with firewall protection
- **HTTPS/TLS:** All API communications use encrypted connections

### Organizational Safeguards

- **Limited Access:** Only 2-3 technical staff have database access
- **No Manual Exports:** Participant data is never manually downloaded or exported
- **Confidentiality Agreements:** All team members bound by confidentiality obligations
- **Data Processing Agreement:** Written agreement with JITO defining security requirements

### WhatsApp Business API Security

- **Meta-Managed Infrastructure:** WhatsApp Business API hosted securely by Meta
- **No Message Storage by Meta:** Meta cannot read message content due to encryption
- **Verified Business Account:** We use official WhatsApp Business API (not unofficial methods)

**Limitations:**  
While we implement robust security, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security against all threats including:
- Advanced persistent cyberattacks
- Insider threats beyond our control
- Catastrophic infrastructure failures
- Government surveillance or legal demands

**In the Event of a Breach:**  
See Section 10 for our data breach notification procedures.

## 9. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS (INDIA DPDP ACT 2023)

Under the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)**, you have the following rights:

### 1. Right to Access
Request confirmation of what personal data we hold about you and obtain a copy.

### 2. Right to Correction
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

### 3. Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)
Request deletion of your personal data before the scheduled deletion date (Feb 28, 2026).

### 4. Right to Data Portability
Request your data in a structured, commonly used format.

### 5. Right to Grievance Redressal
File a complaint with us if you believe your data rights have been violated.

### 6. Right to Nominate
Nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity.
